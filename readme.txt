The ZipZap payment module for WP e-Commerce, WooCommerce and eShop allows your shopping cart to quickly, securely accept payment in cash.

== Description ==
Shop at all of your favorite websites: get the hottest gadgets, find that perfect pair of shoes you've had on your wish-list. Add the items to your cart and proceed to checkout as you normally would, only this time you're paying with cash. When you choose to pay with cash at check out, you will be given the option of selecting the closest payment center for added convenience and ease. With hundreds of thousands of payment centers around the world, shopping online with cash has never been more straightforward. When you visit the payment center, present the payment slip and pay with cash. We then notify the merchant and your purchase is sent right along. Simple? Easy? Of course it is: it's how we do things.

Why Cash?

*Lots of Location
*Ditch Your Card
*Identity Protection
*Safe & secure
*Instant Payment
*Free Account


PS: You'll need an ZipZap Merchant ID and ZipZap HashCode(https://www.cashpayment.com/Public/MerchantSignUp) to use it. 

== Installation ==

Upload the ZipZap plugin to your WP-eCommerce, WooCommerce, or eShop site, Activate it, then enter your Merchant ID, HashCode and ZipZap Order Limit (obtained after creating an account at https://cashpayment.com/).
